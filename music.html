Sound Experiments & Noise

MANIFESTO // SONIC PHILOSOPHY

Ideas

These albums document my exploration of entropy through sound. Each recording captures the moment where structure collapses into chaos, where melody dissolves into texture.

PROCESS NOTES

Hardware feedback loops using cheap semi-modualr Synthesizers, bedroom mixing desks, some weird effects, and analog processing. Everything is performed live in single takes—no overdubs, always try to fuse genres using bass guitar. The errors are the composition.

LISTENING INSTRUCTIONS

Play loud through speakers, not headphones. Let the sound interact with your room. Notice how the noise fills space, how feedback oscillates, how silence creates tension.

"Forget all the equipment, forget the music, at the end of the day it's just literally frequencies and their effects on your brain. That's what's everyone's essentially after. -Aphex Twins"
