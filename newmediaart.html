Artist Statement

ARTIST STATEMENT

I love Touchdesigner. New media art is the exploration of failure states—the moments when technology reveals its seams, when the digital mask slips to expose raw data beneath. These videos document that revelation.

Each piece begins with a system: audio feedback, video compression, signal interference. I set the parameters, then let entropy take over. The results are unpredictable, unrepeatable, authentic.

The glitch is not an error. It's a glimpse of truth in a system designed to hide itself.
